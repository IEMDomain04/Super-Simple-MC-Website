This website provides information on Minecraft, covering the general blocks, characters, and the game's creators.

Minecraft is a widely popular sandbox video game featuring a blocky, procedurally generated 3D world. Developed by Mojang Studios, it allows players to explore, gather resources, and build structures in a creatively open environment. With diverse gameplay modes, including survival and creative, Minecraft has gained global acclaim for its unique blend of exploration, creativity, and adventure since its release in 2011. Minecraft Official Website

Mojang is the Swedish video game development studio responsible for creating Minecraft, one of the most popular and influential sandbox games globally. Acquired by Microsoft in 2014, Mojang continues to develop and support Minecraft, allowing players to explore and create their virtual worlds using a variety of blocks and game mechanics..
